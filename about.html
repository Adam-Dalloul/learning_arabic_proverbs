With a passion for Computer Science, language, and culture, I created “Learning Arabic Proverbs”, an app that aims to enhance the fluency of Arabic learners’ language skills. In order to gain fluency in the Arabic language, not only are fundamental topics necessary to understand, but it is also necessary to know how to utilize proverbs. Proverbs are crucial in Arabic as they are utilized constantly in daily conversation. Without an understanding of them, fluency of the language cannot be established. That is why L.A.P. (Learning Arabic Proverbs) was established, to help Arabic learners to master the language and allow them to fulfill their dreams of Arabic fluency.
