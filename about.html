As a high school student at Eastside Preparatory School, I'm really into computer science and business. I've been part of different programs that focus on business, so I've learned a lot about how it works. I'm planning to major in computer science and want to use what I know to make a difference in the tech world. I came up with the idea for "Learning Arabic Proverbs" because I believe that understanding cultural things, like Arabic proverbs, can help people learn the language better and also get to know more about Arabic heritage. The app is all about making language learning fun and helping people appreciate the wisdom in Arabic proverbs. I'm excited to bring my love for tech and business into this project and hopefully make a positive impact in the language learning and cultural appreciation scene.
